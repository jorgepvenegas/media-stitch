Migration Strategy

How to migrate without breaking everything at once?

A

Parallel app

New Vue app in `src/photowalk/web/vue-app/`. Keep old `assets/` serving at different endpoint. Flip routing once Vue is complete.

✅ No risk to existing app
❌ Duplicated backend wiring
❌ Two things to maintain

B

Gradual component swap

Build Vue app alongside old app.js. Swap one panel at a time — sidebar first, then sync, then timeline. Old app.js shrinks as Vue grows.

✅ Incremental, visible progress
✅ Can test each panel independently
❌ Hybrid state during transition

C

Big bang rewrite

Build full Vue app in `vue-app/`. Only swap when 100% feature-complete. Old app.js deleted at swap.

✅ Clean break, no hybrid state
✅ Full Vue benefits from day one
❌ Long time without visible payoff

My recommendation: B (Gradual swap)
Lets you validate the Vue approach incrementally. Panel-by-panel means you can rollback one panel without losing everything.
